Civil Works

Civil works typically refer to construction projects that are related to infrastructure and public works. These projects involve the design, construction, and maintenance of structures.

Learn more

Structural Works

Structural works involve the design, construction, and maintenance of the load-bearing elements of buildings and other structures. Structural works ensure that buildings and infrastructure are safe, stable, and able to withstand various forces such as gravity, wind, and seismic activity.

Learn more

Architectural Works

Architectural works pertain to the design and aesthetic aspects of buildings and structures. This encompasses the planning, layout, style, and visual appeal of a construction project.

Learn more

Electrical Works

Electrical works involve the installation, maintenance, and repair of electrical systems within buildings, infrastructure, and equipment. This includes wiring, lighting fixtures, outlets, switches, circuit breakers, electrical panels, and other components that facilitate the distribution and utilization of electricity.

Learn more

Mechanical Works

Mechanical works encompass the installation, maintenance, and repair of mechanical systems within buildings, infrastructure, and machinery. This includes heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) systems, plumbing systems, fire protection systems, elevators, escalators, and other mechanical components.

Learn more

Fit-out Works

Fit out works involve the customization and interior finishing of a space to meet specific functional and aesthetic requirements. This includes activities such as installing partitions, ceilings, flooring, lighting, fixtures, furniture, and other interior elements to transform a bare structure into a usable and visually appealing environment.

Learn more
